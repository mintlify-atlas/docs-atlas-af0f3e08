---
title: "Connecting"
description: "Power up Clawd Mochi and connect to its built-in WiFi hotspot to open the web controller."
---

Clawd Mochi hosts its own WiFi access point directly from the ESP32. No router, no home network, and no internet connection are needed.

<Note>
  Clawd Mochi creates its own WiFi access point — you don't need an internet connection or home network.
</Note>

## Connect to the web controller

<Steps>
  <Step title="Power via USB-C">
    Plug a USB-C cable into the ESP32 and connect it to any USB charger or power bank. The display will turn on and show the boot splash.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Wait for the boot animation">
    After the splash screen, a logo reveal animation plays. This takes about 3 seconds. Once it finishes, the display shows the WiFi connection info.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open WiFi settings on your device">
    On your phone or computer, open the WiFi settings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Join the ClaWD-Mochi network">
    Connect to the network named **`ClaWD-Mochi`** with the password **`clawd1234`**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Open the web controller">
    Open a browser and go to **`http://192.168.4.1`**. The web controller loads immediately.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Tip>
  Any device with a browser works: phone, tablet, laptop. The controller is optimized for mobile screens.
</Tip>

## WiFi details

| Setting | Value |
|---------|-------|
| **SSID** | `ClaWD-Mochi` |
| **Password** | `clawd1234` |
| **IP address** | `192.168.4.1` |
| **Port** | 80 (HTTP) |

<Warning>
  After connecting to ClaWD-Mochi, your device loses internet access while connected. Disconnect from ClaWD-Mochi to restore internet.
</Warning>
